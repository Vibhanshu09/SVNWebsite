Get Involved

Get involved with SVN

The SVN team is committed to action and improving educational provision in every single area we work. We encourage others to take action, get involved, and make their own impact.

Ways to get involved

Partner your School

Inspire your students, visit our schools, and develop teamworking skills.

Donate

Every little makes a big difference. Make a donation.

Volunteer

SVN volunteers are helping change lives right now.

Other ways to get involved

This is a real opportunity to do something special with an enduring legacy. You are invited to be part of the SVN journey. Check out the following and commit to one – or several – that work for you:

  • Know a teacher? Pour them a glass of something nice and ask them if they would like to partner SVN school in 2018. Each partnership is bespoke and is mutually beneficial to those involved, and there is no direct cost to the school.
  • Volunteer for 30 mins. Thursday 5th December is International Volunteering Day. Find half an hour to champion the SVN cause.
  • Join us in 2016. The SVN Council is a group of highly active volunteers who regularly meet, both virtually and face-to-face, to share their expertise and give time to support SVN. Drop info@shantividyaniketan.org a line if you would like to join the Council in 2016.
  • Get active on social media and give SVN a plug or two with your friends and contacts; meanwhile please join us via facebook and twitter (see below for more options).
  • Tell others about SVN. Email a link to this page to friends, family and colleagues. And ask them to visit our school once.
